## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your first doc">
    Click `New Document` and fill out the form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a folder">
    Use `New Folder` to group related docs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move docs to folder">
    Drag and drop documents into your folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite team">
    Use `Share` to add teammates and set their roles.
  </Step>
</Steps>
