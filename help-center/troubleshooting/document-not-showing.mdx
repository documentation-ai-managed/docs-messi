---
title: Document Not Showing Up
description: Fix missing or invisible documents in Messi.
---

## Problem

- You created a doc, but it's missing from the list.

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh the page">
    Sometimes updates need a manual refresh. Hit `F5` or reload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check filters">
    Ensure you don't have filters (folders/tags) hiding the file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm permissions">
    Verify you have access to view the document.
  </Step>
</Steps>
